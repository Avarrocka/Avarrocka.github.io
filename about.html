About Me

Hi, I'm Brian.

Hi, welcome!

My name is Brian Chen and I am a first-year student at the University of Toronto, pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science.

I've been coding since I first took a computer science class in Grade 10, and I haven't looked back. I've done Web Development, Software Engineering, Android Development, and Game Development. Some of the projects and other things that I've worked on are avaliable on my GitHub, and on the Projects page of this website.

I love playing and creating games. I'm currently working on a version of Bomberman to familiarize myself with Unity, as well as looking into how to start with Unreal Engine.

I occasionally also do contest programming, on websites such as TopCoder, USACO, and OpenKattis, as well as discuss contest problems with my friends.
